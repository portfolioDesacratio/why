why why?

we started with a simple question. why do we wear what we wear? why do trends dictate identity? why can't clothing be both art and utility?

why? is not just a brand — it's a philosophy. every piece is designed with intention, crafted for those who move through life with purpose. we strip away the unnecessary until only essence remains.

"in a world of more, dare to ask why."
